Edamame

fun flirty beans

this may come as a surprise, but edamame is actually beans. that's right, it's BEANS. But these aren't your regular plain old dumpy beans that any chump can pour out of a can, these are fresh delicious SOY BEANS, yes that's right, SOY BEANS ladies and gentlemen, chok-full of protein and vitamins and FIBER. these make a great appetizer to split with a hot date! share a pod like lady and the tramp and watch the magic happen in real time!

Ingredients

  • edamame
  • salt
  • chili oil

Directions

  1. heat a pot of water to a boil
  2. place beans in a steamer basket over the boiling water
  3. steam the beans until they are bright green and tender
  4. remove from heat and salt to taste
  5. drench in chili oil for an extra blast of flavor
